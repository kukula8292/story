Percy Jackson & the Olympians
Rick Riordan · 2005–2009

Percy Jackson discovers he is the son of a Greek god and must navigate a modern world filled with monsters, demigods, and Olympian drama. Bursting with humour, heart, and mythology — one of the most beloved fantasy series of all time. These two books (Book 1 & Book 4) are standout entries in the five-book saga.

Greek Mythology Adventure Middle Grade Award-Winning

The Mortal Instruments
Cassandra Clare · 2007–2014

Shadowhunters, demons, warlocks, and forbidden love — set in a hidden world alongside our own. Clary Fray's journey from ordinary teen to powerful Shadowhunter spans six gripping books. City of Bones opens this rich world; City of Heavenly Fire delivers the explosive, emotional conclusion to the entire saga at over 725 pages.

Urban Fantasy YA Fiction Romance NYT Bestseller
